Our Story

At Food Paradise, we believe in bringing people together through the love of food. Our restaurant was founded with a simple mission: to offer the authentic flavors of Bangladesh to the people of London. We know how much it means to enjoy a home-cooked meal when you're far from home, which is why our menu is carefully crafted to transport you back to the streets of Dhaka, Sylhet, Chittagong, and beyond.

Our team is passionate about cooking and dedicated to delivering the highest quality Bangladeshi dishes with the freshest ingredients. From classic biryanis to mouthwatering kebabs, we take pride in every dish we prepare. Whether you're looking for comfort food or an exciting culinary adventure, Food Paradise is the place where you can experience the heart of Bangladesh.
